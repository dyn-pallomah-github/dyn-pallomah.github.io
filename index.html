Raider Legend
>  Visit

A man was heartbroken, after losing his family, properties and home in an unfortunate earthquake.
The man was broke, starving and begging people in the other part of the country for food and money.

But no one lent a helping hand. Everyone passed by looked at him with pity and moved on with their lives.

"I have nothing to lose," he thought to himself, standing in front of a door, the Master Raider's home.
He shaved his hair, replaced his worn out clothes, and wore a mask, ready to survive by his own hands.

Thus, a legend was born.
